About Us

HandiConnect is a business registry that connects local informal businesses providing good quality services to clients and customers.

Are you a small business looking for clients within your neighbourhood that require your skills and services? HandiConnect allows you to advertise your skills and services to clients.

Are you looking for a free-lancer to do work but not sure who is the best person for the job?

HandiConnect provides you with information of where good quality and affordable services are available within your area.

Do you want to give recognition to free-lancers who do great work? HandiConnect is the platform just for that.

Are you tired of substandard services provided? HandiConnect is your answer.
